What can we do for you

We have experience in remodeling and building the electrical for grocery stores like Safeway, as well as heating and air-conditioning controls in commercial buildings. We've also done fire alarm systems, security systems, backup generators for both residential and large commercial buildings.
Another area is upgrading lighting to newer LED lights which can save commercial customers thousands in electrical bills. Much of this can get paid for through the Energy Trust OF Oregon tax rebates at both the Federal and the State level. We also do lots of service work and small projects, new services on houses and electrical remodels as well including computer and phone cabling for commercial customers.
